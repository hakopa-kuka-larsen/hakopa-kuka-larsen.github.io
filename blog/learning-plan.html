Expectations of the Bootcamp Team

I expect the bootcamp team to be open, communicative, and patient. I also expect the coordinators and facilitators to clearly lay out tasks and be available to clarify concepts both during and, when possible, outside of course hours.

Commitment to Learning

I am here at Dev Academy to learn how to code. Coding is about creating products for people, by people, and I strive to work collaboratively—pushing myself and my peers to grow. I will treat my fellow students and teachers with kindness and patience, and I will listen attentively. I will seek help in a timely manner by reaching out to facilitators when needed.

If I fall behind on a project or am unable to complete something for any reason, I will prioritize honesty and communicate clearly with the people involved so as not to hinder my own or anyone else’s learning. This course is condensed into a short time frame, and I am prepared for the intensity and challenges ahead. In fact, I’m really excited for it.
